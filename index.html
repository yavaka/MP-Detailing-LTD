ABOUT

Welcome to MP Detailing! I'm Mladen Petkov, and I'm passionate about providing top-notch detailing services in West Midlands and surrounding area. With 3 years of experience, I deliver professional and personalized care to every vehicle I work on.


At MP Detailing, I use cutting-edge equipment and high-quality products to restore and enhance your vehicle's appearance. From hand washing to paint correction and interior cleaning, I offer comprehensive services tailored to your needs. I prioritize eco-friendly practices whenever possible.


What sets my detailing services apart is the personalized experience I provide. I listen to your goals, ensuring results that surpass expectations. Fully licensed and insured, I maintain the highest standards of integrity, reliability, and customer service.


Choose MP Detailing for exceptional detailing that makes your car shine. Contact me for inquiries or appointments.
